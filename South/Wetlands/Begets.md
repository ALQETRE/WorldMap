## Short Description:
Slightly smaller humanoids with webbed feet They evolved in salt water tropical swamps aka mangrove forests. They live in the [[South Wetlands]], mainly at the [[{south_river}]] delta in [[{wetland_nation}]].

## Visual Description:
Slightly smaller humanoids with webbed feet. Their smaller size enables them to easily traverse the dense greenery found around them.
### Physical Traits:
- Webbed feet to easily walk on muddy/unstable terrain.
- Larger noses and stronger lungs to better breathe in humid environments.
- Better kidneys that can filter salts out much more effectively, so much so they can drink salt water and still get hydrated, but they don't enjoy it, it still tastes bad.

## Style of Living:
They live on platforms built into the mangrove forest.
