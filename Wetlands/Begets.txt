Description:
	Slightly smaller humanoids with webbed feet to
	enable them to easily walk on unstable muddy terrain.
	They evolved in salt water tropical swamps aka mangrove forests.
	Their smaller size enables them to easily travers the dense greenery
	found around them. They have larger noses and stronger lungs to better
	breethe in humid enviroments. They have better kidneys that can filter salts
	out much more effectively, so much so they can drink salt water and still get
	hydrated, they don't enjoy it, it still tastes bad, but in a dire situation
	they can.
